Executive Summary

Platform Operating Model: LIVE, REAL-TIME, CLIENT-DIRECTED
  • NO Pre-Trade approval delays
  • NO mandatory acceptance windows
  • Direct order placement by Client → Immediate execution by Nihao
  • All settlement handled transparently through Nihao intermediation
  • Automated status updates and lifecycle communications
  • Dynamic status dashboard accessible 24/7

Client Journey - Three-Phase Model

Phase 1: Account & Funding

  1. Client completes KYC/AML verification
  2. Fund Escrow Account (deposit EUR/USD)
  3. AML hold period (1-2 business days)
  4. Funds cleared and ready for trading

Phase 2: CEA Marketplace Acquisition

  1. Browse Marketplace CEA (real-time listings)
  2. Place buy order (immediate execution)
  3. CEA transfer initiated from seller
  4. CEA received at Nihao custody (2-3 business days)
  5. Client receives CEA custody confirmation

Phase 3: CEA-to-EUA Swap Execution

  1. Browse Swap Center (real-time swap offers)
  2. Execute swap order (immediate matching)
  3. CEA transferred to swap counterparty
  4. EUA received from counterparty at Nihao
  5. EUA delivered to Client's Union Registry account (T+3 to T+5)
  6. Transaction complete, compliance-ready

Timeline

Bank Transfer Path:

Client's bank sends SWIFT wire

Settlement Failure Protection

Three-Level Settlement Failure Remedies guarantee ZERO financial loss to client.

Level 1: Cure Attempt (First 24 hours)

Action: Investigation + reconnection with counterparty
Cost to Client: EUR 0.00
Success Rate: 95% (most delays resolved within 24 hours)
Timeline: Next business day status update
Outcome: Settlement completes OR escalate to Level 2

Level 2: Secondary Market Remedy (Days 2-5)

Action: Nihao sources replacement CEA/EUA from market
Cost to Client: EUR 0.00
Nihao Absorption: Up to 2% premium on market price
Timeline: 2-3 business days
Outcome: Client receives exact quantity promised OR escalate to Level 3

Level 3: Full Refund (Day 6+)

Action: Full refund of all amounts + waive all fees
Cost to Client: EUR 0.00
Nihao Absorption: 100% of all losses
Timeline: Same day processing
Outcome: Client account fully restored, transaction unwound
