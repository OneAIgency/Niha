SECTION 1: ESTABLISHMENT OF ESCROW ACCOUNT

1.1 Account Structure

(a) Client shall open a segregated bank account at Bank in Client's name with the account designation:

**"Italy Nihao Group Limited – [Client Legal Name] Escrow Account (Segregated)"**

(b) The Escrow Account shall be established within 5 business days of KYC approval.

(c) Bank shall confirm in writing to both Client and Nihao:
    - Account opening and IBAN
    - Segregation status (Client funds separate from Nihao operating accounts)
    - Ring-fencing protection (funds not subject to Nihao creditor claims)
    - Insurance or protection arrangements (per Section 1.3)
    - Access restrictions (Nihao cannot access without Client authorization)

(d) Nihao shall not receive funds directly. All Client deposits flow to the Escrow Account at Bank, held in Client's name exclusively.

1.2 Beneficial Ownership and Control

(a) **Beneficial Owner:** Client is the exclusive beneficial owner of all Escrow Account funds at all times.

(b) **Legal Title:** Funds held in Client's name, not Nihao's name. Bank records shall reflect Client as account holder.

(c) **Control:** Client retains unilateral control over the Escrow Account, including:
    - Right to view account balance 24/7 via Platform
    - Right to request voluntary withdrawal at any time
    - Right to terminate the Escrow Account and receive full return of funds
    - Right to restrict or revoke Nihao's limited agency authority

(d) **Nihao's Role:** Nihao acts solely as agent for limited purposes specified in Section 2, not as principal or fiduciary.

1.3 Ring-Fencing and Creditor Protection

(a) **Segregation:** Bank shall maintain the Escrow Account as legally segregated from:
    - Nihao's operating accounts
    - Nihao's general business funds
    - Any other client accounts

(b) **No Liens:** Nihao's creditors shall have no liens, charges, or security interests in the Escrow Account.

(c) **Bankruptcy Protection:** In the event of Nihao insolvency or bankruptcy:
    - Escrow funds shall NOT be subject to Nihao's creditor claims
    - Escrow funds shall NOT be included in Nihao's bankruptcy estate
    - Escrow funds shall be returned to Client or held by successor custodian
    - Client shall rank as priority secured creditor for Escrow funds

(d) **Bank Representations:** Bank warrants that:
    - Escrow Account is properly segregated in Bank's ledger
    - No liens or encumbrances exist against the account
    - Ring-fencing protections are legally valid and enforceable
    - Segregation status shall be verified annually by external auditors

1.4 Insurance and Protection

(a) Bank shall maintain:
    - Deposit insurance per [Jurisdiction] regulations, OR
    - Alternative protection (e.g., contractual guarantee, letters of credit)
    - Cyber liability insurance (minimum EUR 1,000,000)
    - Professional liability insurance (minimum EUR 1,000,000)

(b) Bank shall provide annual proof of insurance to Client.

(c) If Bank's insurance lapses, Bank shall notify Client within 5 business days and remediate within 10 business days.

(d) Client may request Bank account transfer to alternative financial institution if insurance coverage becomes inadequate.

SECTION 2: LIMITED AGENCY AUTHORITY

2.1 Scope of Agency

Client hereby appoints Nihao as limited agent for the sole and exclusive purpose of executing EUA acquisitions per Trade Confirmations. This agency is LIMITED and specific; Nihao may NOT act as general agent or take any action outside this scope.

2.2 Authorized Actions

Nihao is authorized solely to:

(a) **CEA Acquisition:**
    - Issue purchase orders to Chinese CEA sellers
    - Negotiate CEA prices within Client guidelines (maximum price limits)
    - Enter into swap agreements with counterparties for CEA-to-EUA conversion
    - Coordinate with China National Carbon Registry for CEA registration

(b) **Escrow Account Payments:**
    - Authorize wire transfers from Escrow Account for:
        - EUA purchase costs
        - Facilitation Fees
        - Bank charges (SWIFT, registry, custodian)
    - Only upon conditions specified in Trade Confirmation

(c) **Registry Instructions:**
    - Instruct Union Registry to transfer completed EUA to Client's Registry Account
    - Provide registry transfer documentation
    - Coordinate with registry for account verification

(d) **Counterparty Coordination:**
    - Communicate with CEA suppliers and EUA sellers
    - Execute agreements with counterparties (within limits)
    - Monitor performance and resolve disputes

(e) **Settlement Coordination:**
    - Monitor settlement timeline (T+0 to T+5)
    - Coordinate with counterparties and registry
    - Escalate delays and invoke Settlement Failure remedies per Master Agreement

(f) **Limited Document Execution:**
    - Sign Trade Confirmations on behalf of Client
    - Execute agency acknowledgments
    - Provide transaction confirmations to Client
    - Negotiate with counterparties within pre-authorized limits

2.3 Prohibited Actions

Nihao is EXPRESSLY PROHIBITED from:

(a) **Unauthorized Fund Movements:**
    - Taking funds from Escrow Account for any purpose other than specified in Trade Confirmation
    - Using Client funds for Nihao operating expenses or general business
    - Pledging or using Client funds as collateral for Nihao's own borrowings
    - Depositing Client funds in accounts other than designated Escrow Account
    - Commingling Client funds with Nihao's funds

(b) **Prohibited Trading Activities:**
    - Entering derivative contracts, options, forwards, or futures
    - Using margin, leverage, or collateral arrangements
    - Short-selling EUA or CEA
    - Speculative trading beyond scope of Trade Confirmation
    - Using Client funds for Nihao's own account

(c) **Unauthorized Commitments:**
    - Committing Client to any obligation beyond EUA acquisition specified
    - Pledging Client assets
    - Guaranteeing third-party obligations
    - Entering multi-year contracts or long-term commitments
    - Committing Client to any regulatory compliance obligation

(d) **Unauthorized Disclosures:**
    - Revealing Client information to unauthorized third parties
    - Disclosing Client transaction details to counterparties without permission
    - Publishing Client trade information
    - Using Client information for Nihao's marketing

(e) **Account Manipulation:**
    - Changing Client account information without authorization
    - Modifying Union Registry account details
    - Restricting Client's access to Platform or account data
    - Altering transaction records or ledger entries

(f) **Conflicts of Interest:**
    - Transacting with Nihao-related entities at above-market prices
    - Using position as agent to extract improper profits
    - Taking personal benefit from transactions

2.4 Conditions on Agency Authority

(a) **Pre-Authorization Required:** Nihao may only exercise agency authority upon Client's explicit authorization via signed Trade Confirmation.

(b) **Specific Scope:** Authority is limited to the specific EUA quantity, price, and terms stated in Trade Confirmation.

(c) **No Authority to Exceed:** Nihao has no authority to:
    - Purchase greater quantities than authorized
    - Exceed maximum prices stated in Trade Confirmation
    - Commit to extended settlement timelines
    - Accept terms materially different from Trade Confirmation

(d) **Client Revocation:** Client may revoke Nihao's agency authority at any time by written notice to legal@nihaogroup.hk. Revocation effective upon receipt.

(e) **Automatic Termination:** Agency authority terminates upon earliest of:
    - Client's termination of this Agreement
    - Client's account closure
    - Nihao's removal of Client access (for breach or regulatory cause)
    - 3 years from execution (annual renewal required)

2.5 Agency Compliance and Reporting

(a) **Periodic Reports:** Nihao shall provide monthly reports showing:
    - All authorized transactions
    - Exercise of agency authority
    - Compliance with authorization limits
    - Any breaches or exceptions

(b) **Transparency:** Client may audit Nihao's agency performance at any time upon 5 business days' notice.

(c) **Indemnification:** Nihao indemnifies Client for any losses arising from Nihao's:
    - Exercise of authority outside scope authorized
    - Breach of agency duties
    - Negligent or reckless exercise of authority
    - Fraud or intentional misconduct

SECTION 3: MINIMUM BALANCE REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Risk Tier Minimums

Client shall maintain minimum Escrow Account balance appropriate to Risk Tier assignment:

| Risk Tier | Minimum Balance | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Tier 1 (Low Risk) | EUR 50,000 | Demonstrates financial commitment; covers 2-3 transactions |
| Tier 2 (Medium Risk) | EUR 25,000 | Demonstrates financial commitment; covers 1-2 transactions |
| Tier 3 (High Risk) | EUR 10,000 | Demonstrates financial commitment; covers 1 transaction |

3.2 Enforcement

(a) **Monitoring:** Nihao shall monitor Escrow Account balance daily.

(b) **Below-Minimum Cure Period:** If balance falls below minimum:
    - Client has 5 business days to cure by depositing additional funds
    - Nihao shall provide 24-hour notice when balance drops below minimum
    - Notice shall be sent via email and Platform alert

(c) **Non-Cure Consequences:**
    - Upon expiration of 5-day cure period, if balance still below minimum:
    - Client's account trading privileges are immediately suspended
    - No new Pre-Trade Confirmations may be issued
    - Pending Transactions may continue to completion
    - Account remains suspended until minimum restored

(d) **Restoration:** Account trading privileges automatically restored upon Client's restoration of balance to minimum.

3.3 Minimum Balance Adjustments

(a) Nihao may adjust minimum balance requirements upon 30 days' notice if:
    - Client's Risk Tier classification changes
    - Market conditions or regulatory changes warrant adjustment
    - Nihao's operational costs materially increase

(b) Material increases (>50%) require Client approval or opportunity to terminate Agreement.

(e) **Historical Archive:**
    - Complete transaction history (1-year rolling display)

(b) **Accuracy Verification:**
    - Ledger reconciled daily with Bank statements

    - Annual audit by external auditors

(c) **Error Correction:** Any discrepancies identified within 5 business days, with root cause investigation and correction to ledger.

(d) **Client Verification:** Client may request detailed supporting documentation for any ledger entry within 10 business days of entry.

4.3 Ledger Authentication

(a) Ledger entries are digitally signed with timestamp.

(b) Ledger is maintained on encrypted, secure server with backup systems.

(c) Ledger cannot be retroactively modified; all changes create new audit trail entries.

(d) Client receives monthly ledger statement via email in PDF format.

SECTION 5: FUND RETURN PROCEDURES

5.1 Upon Successful Settlement

(a) Upon settlement of EUA (T+5), Nihao shall:
    - Confirm successful delivery to Client's Union Registry Account
    - Calculate final fees due and amounts to be returned
    - Deduct Facilitation Fee and bank charges from Escrow Account
    - Return net remaining balance to Client (or hold for next Transaction per Client preference)

(b) **Automatic Processing:** If Client has authorized automatic fee deduction, fees deducted without further notice.

(c) **Manual Processing:** If Client requires manual approval, Nihao requests authorization via email within 24 hours of settlement confirmation.

(d) **Return Timeline:** Net balance available for withdrawal or automatic return to Client's bank account within 1 business day of settlement.

5.2 Voluntary Withdrawal

(a) **Request Process:** Client may request withdrawal of funds at any time by emailing withdrawals@nihaogroup.hk with:
    - Requested withdrawal amount
    - Destination IBAN / bank account details
    - Account holder name (must match bank records)
    - Confirmation that no pending Transactions depend on funds

(b) **Validation:** Nihao Finance shall validate:
    - Client identity (match to KYC records)
    - Sufficient available balance (excluding minimum or pending Transactions)
    - No regulatory holds or disputes
    - Destination account authorized for Client

(c) **Processing:** Upon validation (typically within 1 business day):
    - Wire transfer initiated from Escrow Account
    - Client receives confirmation email with wire details and reference number
    - Bank typically completes transfer within 2-5 business days (depending on receiving bank)

(d) **Fees:** EUR 25 SWIFT charge per withdrawal, deducted from withdrawal amount (unless waived per Section 5.4).

(e) **Timing:** Withdrawals requested before 14:00 HK time processed same business day; after 14:00 processed next business day.

5.3 Upon Account Termination

(a) **Termination Notice:** Upon Client's termination notice or Nihao's termination, 30-day transition period begins.

(b) **Final Accounting (Day 25):**
    - All pending Transactions allowed to complete
    - No new Transactions accepted
    - Final reconciliation prepared showing:
        - Total Escrow balance
        - Less: Outstanding Facilitation Fees
        - Less: Disputed amounts (pending resolution)
        - Less: Regulatory holds or sanctions freezes
        - Equals: **NET RETURN BALANCE**

(c) **Fund Return (Day 30):**
    - Wire transfer of NET RETURN BALANCE to Client's designated bank account
    - Return must complete within 5 business days of termination date
    - EUR 25 SWIFT fee deducted (unless dispute situation, then waived)
    - Confirmation email provided with wire details

(d) **Remaining Balance:** If disputes remain unresolved, those amounts held until resolution, then returned.

(e) **Account Closure:** Escrow Account closed once balance reaches EUR 0.00 and all disputes resolved.

5.4 Interest on Delayed Returns

(a) **Delay Compensation:** If fund return delayed beyond promised timeframe due to Nihao administrative error:
    - Interest: 1% per month (12% per annum) on unreturned balance
    - Calculated daily from promised return date to actual return date
    - Added to returned balance automatically

(b) **Example:**
    - Promised return date: January 15
    - Actual return date: February 15 (31 days delayed)
    - Balance: EUR 50,000
    - Interest: EUR 50,000 × 1% = EUR 500
    - Client receives: EUR 50,500

(c) **Fee Waiver:** EUR 25 SWIFT fee waived if return delayed >5 business days.

(d) **Calculation:** Interest calculated and added automatically to fund return; no Client request necessary.

SECTION 6: FUND CUSTODY AND SECURITY

6.1 Custodian Standards

(a) Escrow Account shall be held by Bank meeting standards for:
    - Regulated financial institution in acceptable jurisdiction (EU, Hong Kong, Switzerland, Singapore)
    - Minimum 5 years operating history
    - No recent regulatory enforcement actions
    - Credit rating of A- or higher (if rated)
    - Audited financial statements showing adequate capitalization

(b) Nihao shall not hold Client funds in:
    - Unregulated or shadow banking entities
    - Cryptocurrency exchanges or digital wallets
    - Personal bank accounts or informal arrangements

6.2 Control Procedures

(a) **Four-Eyes Principle:** Any withdrawal from Escrow Account requires authorization from two independent Nihao officers:
    - Officer 1: Validates transaction authorization (Trade Confirmation match)
    - Officer 2: Approves fund transfer execution
    - Both officers must be Nihao employees (not third-party vendors)

(b) **Authorized Officers:** Nihao shall maintain list of authorized officers, provided to Client and Bank.

(c) **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** All Escrow Account access requires 2FA (password + SMS code / authenticator app).

(d) **Digital Signatures:** All wire transfer instructions digitally signed by two authorized officers.

(e) **Access Logs:** Complete access logs maintained for 7 years, audited monthly:
    - Date and time of access
    - User ID and name
    - Action performed (view, initiate, approve)
    - Amount and destination
    - IP address and authentication method

(f) **Third-Party Exclusion:** No third-party service providers (accountants, consultants, etc.) have access to Escrow Account or authority to withdraw funds.

6.3 Fund Segregation Verification

(a) **Annual Segregation Audit:** External auditors shall verify annually that:
    - Escrow Account maintained separately from Nihao operating accounts
    - No Nihao creditors have liens or claims
    - Bank confirmation of segregation status
    - Ring-fencing protections legally valid and enforceable

(b) **Bank Confirmation:** Bank shall provide written confirmation of segregation status:
    - Within 30 days of account opening
    - Annually thereafter
    - Upon Client's written request (within 10 business days)

(c) **Audit Access:** Client may request independent audit of Escrow Account segregation at Client's expense (Client reimburses audit fees).

(d) **Remediation:** If segregation found inadequate, Nihao has 30 days to:
    - Transfer funds to alternative custodian, OR
    - Return funds to Client, OR
    - Obtain legal opinion and remediation plan

6.4 Cybersecurity and Data Protection

(a) **Encryption:** All Client data encrypted using industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2 or higher, AES-256).

(b) **Network Security:**
    - Multi-layer firewall protection
    - Intrusion detection and prevention systems
    - Regular security patching and updates
    - Network segregation for critical systems

(c) **Access Controls:**
    - Strong password requirements (minimum 12 characters, complexity)
    - Role-based access controls (least privilege principle)
    - Session timeout (30 minutes inactivity)
    - Login attempt limits (5 failures = 30-minute lockout)

(d) **Annual Security Audits:**
    - Penetration testing (simulated cyber attack) annually
    - Vulnerability assessment quarterly
    - Security audit report provided to Client annually
    - Third-party security certification (ISO 27001 or equivalent)

(e) **Incident Response:**
    - Cyber incident response plan documented
    - 24-hour incident notification procedure
    - Client notified within 24 hours of any security incident
    - Investigation and remediation within 72 hours
    - Post-incident report within 10 business days

6.5 Insurance and Liability

(a) **Nihao Insurance:**
    - Cyber liability insurance: minimum EUR 1,000,000
    - Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance: minimum EUR 500,000
    - Directors and Officers liability: minimum EUR 1,000,000
    - All policies renewed annually, proof provided to Client

(b) **Bank Insurance:**
    - Deposit insurance or equivalent protection
    - Professional liability insurance
    - Proof of insurance provided annually

(c) **Insurance Verification:** Client may request proof of insurance at any time within 10 business days.

(d) **Insurance Lapse:** If insurance lapses:
    - Bank/Nihao notify Client within 5 business days
    - Remediation within 10 business days (obtain new coverage or return funds)
    - If lapse > 30 days, Client may terminate Agreement and receive full return of funds

7.1 Backup Systems

(a) Nihao maintains:
    - Hot backup systems (real-time replication)
    - Geographic redundancy (backup data center in different location)
    - Quarterly backup recovery testing
    - 24-hour Recovery Time Objective (RTO)
    - Backup logs and records maintained 7 years

(b) If primary systems fail >24 hours:
    - Backup systems activated automatically
    - Client notified within 2 hours of outage detection
    - Daily updates provided until restoration
    - Post-incident report within 10 business days

7.2 Successor Custodian

(a) **Contingency Plan:** If Nihao becomes insolvent or ceases operations:
    - All Escrow funds transferred to successor qualified custodian
    - Successor custodian selected pre-emptively and documented
    - Client notified within 24 hours of transfer
    - Successor maintains same segregation and protection standards

(b) **Successor Criteria:**
    - Regulated financial institution
    - Minimum A- credit rating
    - Prior agreement to accept Client accounts
    - Same or better security/insurance standards
    - Bank and Client consent obtained in advance

(c) **Pre-Arranged Agreement:** Nihao maintains written agreement with successor custodian, reviewed annually.

SECTION 8: TERMINATION AND ACCOUNT CLOSURE

8.1 Termination of Agency Authority

(a) **Client Termination of Agency:** Client may revoke Nihao's agency authority at any time by written notice via email to legal@nihaogroup.hk.

(b) **Effect of Revocation:**
    - Nihao may no longer execute new Transactions
    - Existing authorizations (already-signed Trade Confirmations) continue to completion
    - Fund return procedures per Section 5 apply

(c) **Automatic Termination:**
    - Agency authority terminates if Client account closed
    - Agency authority terminates if Client Agreement terminated
    - Agency authority terminates 3 years from execution unless renewed

8.2 Account Termination and Closure

(a) **Termination Notice:** Either party may terminate this Escrow Agreement with 30 days' written notice to the other.

(b) **Upon Termination:**
    - 30-day transition period begins
    - Pending Transactions allowed to complete
    - No new Transactions accepted
    - Final accounting prepared per Section 5.3
    - NET RETURN BALANCE returned within 5 business days of termination date

(c) **Immediate Termination Triggers (without 30-day notice):**
    - Client placed on sanctions list
    - AML/KYC violation discovered
    - Client breach of this Agreement not cured within 10 days
    - Regulatory authority directive to close account
    - Client threat of litigation or regulatory complaint

(d) Upon immediate termination for Client breach:
    - Account suspended immediately
    - Final accounting prepared within 5 business days
    - Funds returned within 5 business days (less outstanding fees and disputed amounts)

8.3 Closure Procedures

(a) **Final Accounting** (Day 5 before termination):
    - All transactions recorded and settled
    - All fees calculated and documented
    - Disputes identified and documented
    - NET RETURN BALANCE calculated

(b) **Fund Return** (Day 30):
    - Wire transfer of NET RETURN BALANCE to Client's designated account
    - Completion within 5 business days of termination
    - EUR 25 SWIFT fee deducted (unless dispute situation)

(c) **Account Closure** (Upon EUR 0.00 balance):
    - Bank closes Escrow Account
    - Final statement provided to Client
    - Escrow Agent-Bank Escrow Agreement terminated

(d) **Record Retention** (7 years):
    - Nihao retains all records relating to account for 7 years
    - Records provided to Client or regulatory authorities upon request
    - After 7 years, records securely destroyed
