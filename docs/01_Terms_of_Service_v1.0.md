SECTION 4: PLATFORM RULES AND TRADING MECHANICS

4.1 Platform Access

(a) Client shall access the Platform using unique login credentials provided at account activation.

(c) Client shall not:
    - Share login credentials with third parties

    - Conduct speculative or short-selling activities
    - Use margin, leverage, or derivatives products
    - Disclose confidential Platform information

(d) Nihao may suspend or terminate Client's account for violation of these rules.

4.2 Trading Hours

(a) The Platform accepts Pre-Trade Confirmations and Trade Confirmations during the following hours:
    - Monday–Friday: 08:00–18:00 Hong Kong Time (HKT)
    - Excluding Hong Kong public holidays and EU public holidays

(b) Orders submitted outside trading hours shall be queued and processed during the next trading session.

(c) Pre-Trade Confirmations submitted during non-trading hours shall be deemed valid from the next trading session opening.

4.3 Order Types: Pre-Trade vs. Trade Confirmation

(a) **Pre-Trade Confirmation:**
    - Non-binding preliminary offer
    - Indicates indicative pricing for EUA
    - Validity: 24 hours from issuance
    - Client may ACCEPT, REJECT, or REQUEST MODIFICATION
    - No financial obligation until acceptance
    - Intended to allow Client time for internal approval

(b) **Trade Confirmation:**
    - Legally binding transaction document
    - Created when Client accepts Pre-Trade Confirmation
    - Binding upon electronic signature by both parties
    - Irrevocable except for 2-hour post-signature cancellation right (if CEA acquisition not commenced)
    - Each Trade Confirmation represents a distinct Transaction

4.4 Binding Transaction Creation

(a) A binding Transaction is created when:
    - Client receives Pre-Trade Confirmation
    - Client digitally signs Pre-Trade Confirmation within 24-hour validity period
    - Nihao counter-signs to create Trade Confirmation
    - Both signatures are electronically timestamped on Platform

(b) Upon creation of binding Trade Confirmation:
    - Client's Escrow Account is encumbered for the full transaction amount plus fees
    - CEA acquisition process commences automatically
    - No further cancellations are permitted except per Section 4.5

(c) Trade Confirmation shall include:
    - Unique Transaction ID (format: TXN-XXXX-YYYY-ZZZZ)
    - EUA quantity in whole units
    - Unit price in EUR
    - Total consideration
    - Facilitation Fee amount
    - Settlement Date (T+5)
    - Client's Union Registry Account destination

4.5 Two-Hour Post-Signature Cancellation Right

(a) Client may cancel a Trade Confirmation within 2 hours of signature if:
    - CEA acquisition has NOT been commenced in China
    - Client submits cancellation request via email to trading@nihaogroup.hk
    - Request is received and processed within the 2-hour window

(b) Upon valid cancellation request:
    - All pending actions are halted immediately
    - Nihao shall refund 100% of deposited funds within 2 business days
    - Facilitation Fee shall be waived
    - No penalties or additional charges apply

(c) After 2-hour window expires:
    - Trade Confirmation is irrevocable
    - CEA acquisition process is underway
    - No cancellations permitted
    - Client must proceed to Settlement or invoke Settlement Failure remedies if delivery fails
